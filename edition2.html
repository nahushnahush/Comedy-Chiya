About The Event

Comedy With Chiya Edition 1 was our very first stand-up comedy night. The event brought together comedians and audiences for an evening full of laughter, storytelling, and community bonding over a cup of chiya. This edition marked the beginning of our journey to build a comedy community in Nepal.
